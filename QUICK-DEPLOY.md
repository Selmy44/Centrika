### Step 1: Open Netlify Drop
Click this link: **https://app.netlify.com/drop**

### Step 2: Create Free Account
- Click "Sign up"
- Use your email, GitHub, or GitLab
- Takes 30 seconds

### Step 3: Drag Your Folder
1. Go back to **https://app.netlify.com/drop**
